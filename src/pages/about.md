## What This Is

Creative Mind Habits is where I document what I'm making and learning. It's part build log, part learning archive, part idea collection.

I make things, learn new skills, and share what works. No polish required, just progress.

<div style="height: 2rem;"></div>

## What You'll Find

- **Build logs** for projects I'm working on (web apps, classroom tools, this blog)
- **Learning documentation** (touch typing, Korean, video editing, project management)
- **Systems and workflows** that make work easier
- **Book notes** and what I'm taking from them
- **Tools and templates** I've built along the way

<div style="height: 2rem;"></div>

## If You're Here, You Probably…

- Like making things and learning new skills
- Appreciate seeing the process, not just the result
- Build systems that support your work
- Enjoy learning random but interesting stuff
